About us

... we plant for life

Thank you for visiting our website!

Bristle specializes in rare tropicals, cactus plants, succulent plants, caudex plants and many species of mesembs and other houseplants.

Besides plants we offer cactus seeds, succulent seeds and many different kinds of exotic seeds.

Do you need soil, containers and any other accessories for the cactus & succulent hobby? We can help you with our selected range of different soil mixtures, containers, fertilizers and more.

We will also buy your old pots, or recycle those that have become unusable.

Bristle has been known for attending a hen party or two where we create bridal bouquets, wedding favours and center pieces while sipping wine together.

We also want to give back to the community, our potting parties are legendary. We pot plants, talk plants and celebrate green life together.

Commercial customers are very important to us that is why we supply large specimen plants for office buildings and also offer a care package where we come back whenever necessary to water, clean the containers and if required replace the plant.
